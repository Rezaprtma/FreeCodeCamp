Kapitan Pattimura

Pattimura | Thomas Matulessy

He is a hero who fought for Maluku against the Dutch VOC. Previously Pattimura was a former sergeant in the British military. in 1816 the British fell on their knees to the Dutch. The return of Dutch colonialism in 1817 was met with stiff challenges from the people. This is due to the bad political, economic and social relations conditions for two centuries. The people of Maluku finally got up to take up arms under the leadership of Kapitan Pattimura.
